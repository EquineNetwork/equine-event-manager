Individual reports
Orders
Every order with customer, items, payment status, totals. Best for transactional bookkeeping.

Which stalls and RV lots were used each night. Useful for venue settlement and post-event analysis.

All customers with contact info and lifetime spend. For mailing lists and CRM exports.
Refund Log
Every refund issued with reason, date, admin user. For compliance and audit trails.
